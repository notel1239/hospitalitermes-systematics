# *Hospitalitermes umbrinus* (Holmgren, 1912)

## Etymology
The species name *umbrinus* refers to the dark coloration of the soldiers.

## Diagnosis
Soldier head capsule dark brown; mandibles with distinct apical teeth; labrum with a deep median cleft. Worker mandibles with four marginal teeth.

## Material examined
**Holotype**: Soldier, Borneo, Sabah, coll. 1911, deposited in NHM London.

**Paratypes**: 10 soldiers, 20 workers, same locality.

## Distribution
Known from Borneo (Sabah, Sarawak) and Sumatra.

## Remarks
This species is commonly found in lowland rainforests, nesting in rotten logs.
